
Medical Aid Cost Prediction Report

1. Introduction
Predicting medical insurance charges using customer demographics and lifestyle data.

2. Data Summary
- Clean data, no missing values.
- Features: age, sex, BMI, children, smoker, region, charges.

3. EDA Findings
- Smokers have significantly higher charges.
- Positive correlation between age and charges.
- Visualisations: Pairplot, Heatmap, Boxplot.

4. Model Building
- Linear Regression model.
- Data split: 80% training, 20% testing.

5. Evaluation
- R^2 Score: 0.79
- RMSE: ~6022
- MAE: ~4207

6. Conclusion
- Model predicts insurance charges reliably.
- Future work: consider regularization and larger datasets.
